    the 4th International Conference on Applied Chemistry" (4th ICAC)

    On behalf of the International Society of Applied Chemistry and Community Development (ISAC-CD) and Sohag University, it is a great pleasure to invite you to attend the 4th International Conference on Applied Chemistry" (4th ICAC) under the theme "New Trend On Applied Chemistry" to be held in Hurghada - Egypt, from April 3-6, 2021.

    The abstract submissions are still accepted for oral and poster presentations if you would like to disseminate your ideas. Hurghada is located on the Red Sea Coast and considers as one of the famous tourist areas. It would take a substantial amount of time to visit everything in this amazing city.

    There will be ample opportunity included in the conference for enjoying the beauty of Hurghada city, including Cruises, and Diving in the Red Sea. Further information regarding registration and abstract submission are available from here

    Topics

    The main topics covering the hot interdisciplinary issues in applied chemistry and nano science are:

    • Nanotechnology and Materials Science
    • Catalysis and Polymer Science
    • Photocatalysis and Photoelectrochemical cells
    • Dye-Sensitized and Pervoskite solar cells
    • Chemical Education
    • Geochemistry and Mineral Chemistry
    • Green and Environmental Chemistry
    • Medicinal & Pharmaceutical Chemistry
    • Organic & Bio-Chemistry and Natural Products
    • Inorganic Chemistry and Analytical Chemistry
    • Physical and Theoretical Chemistry
    • Environmental Chemistry and Renewable Energy
    • Industrial Chemistry and Petroleum Chemistry
    • Food and Agricultural Chemistry
